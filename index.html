About Me

My Background

I'm Amita Kumar, a Product Manager with 6+ years building products across Ad Tech, Gaming, and SaaS. I've consistently shipped features that drive measurable impact, from AI-driven player segmentation that lifted revenue 12% to automated reconciliation systems that cut manual work by 60%.

What sets me apart is that I don't just manage AI features. I build prototypes myself using Claude, n8n, and Cursor to validate concepts before committing engineering resources. This hands-on approach means I move faster, write better specs, and have more credible conversations with technical teams.

My focus areas include intelligent automation, workflow optimization, and AI-assisted decision systems, built with clear success metrics and real user impact.

Product Philosophy

Evaluation-First Development

Define the rubric before you ship the feature. Without clear evals, you're flying blind. I build scoring frameworks first, then iterate until the product consistently meets them.

Agentic Workflows Over One-Shot Prompts

The best AI products orchestrate multiple specialized agents, not single LLM calls. I design systems where agents handle research, synthesis, and validation in coordinated loops.

User Impact Over Technical Novelty

AI should solve real problems, not showcase capabilities. I prioritize features that demonstrably reduce friction or enable new possibilities, measured through clear metrics.
